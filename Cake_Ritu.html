Cake Recipe

Ingredients

  • creamed butter instead of melted
  • cake flour instead of all-purpose flour
  • white sugar instead of brown sugar + white
  • just egg whites all guarantee a HUGE textural difference.

Cake Flour: Lighter than all-purpose flour, cake flour produces a soft crumb.

Baking Powder & Baking Soda: The two add plenty of lift.

Egg Whites: Egg whites create the fluffiest white cake and they’re doing the same job here. Egg yolks, though excellent for moisture, weigh a cake down. Sometimes they’re imperative for flavor, such as in my yellow cake recipe, but we don’t need them in this fluffy sprinkle cake. The sour cream makes up for the lost moisture.
